A paragraph is a group of related sentences—often four to six—that develop a single, central idea or topic, usually introduced by a topic sentence. It acts as a cohesive unit of text, structuring essays and reports by organizing main points, supporting evidence, and analysis, typically separated by spacing or indentation.
